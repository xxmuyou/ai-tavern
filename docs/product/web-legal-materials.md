# CharaPal Web Legal Materials

Last updated: June 18, 2026

## Summary

CharaPal exposes a small set of launch-facing policy materials on web:

- Terms of Service: `/terms`
- Privacy Policy: `/privacy`
- Refund Policy: `/refund`
- Safety and Content Rules: `/safety`
- Contact and Support: `/contact`

In-product web entry points open these documents in a floating dialog so users do not have to leave the current screen. The public URLs still exist for payment provider review, OAuth review, external support links, and direct user access.

## Contact

The public support email for these materials is `admin@aiappsbox.com`.

## Scope

- This is a web-only launch support pass.
- Native legal entry points are not part of this pass.
- The documents are product-ready drafts for launch support, but they are not a substitute for formal legal review.
- Terms, privacy, refund, safety, and contact copy are stored in one shared content source so dialogs and public pages stay consistent.
